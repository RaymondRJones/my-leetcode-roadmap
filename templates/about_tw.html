My Journey

Landed my first real-world software engineering experience at a major financial institution. Learned about production systems and enterprise-level development.

Software Engineer at Fortune 1 Company

Achieved my goal of working at a Fortune 1 company, applying everything I learned along the way. Now I want to help others follow a similar path.

Why I Built This Site

I spent 6 hours creating this website and condensing all the information from my decade of experience because I want to help. I've been exactly where you are - confused about what to learn, overwhelmed by the options, and unsure about the path forward.

This roadmap represents the exact journey I wish I had when I started. Every level, every requirement, every piece of advice comes from real experience and real mistakes I made along the way.
