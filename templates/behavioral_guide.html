STAR Method Framework
Use this structure for all your behavioral stories
Situation

Set the context and background. Where were you? When did this happen?

Task

Describe what you needed to accomplish. What was your responsibility?

Action

Explain the specific steps YOU took. Use "I" statements, not "we".

Result

Share the measurable outcomes. Include metrics and lessons learned.

Pro Tips
Be specific: Use numbers, percentages, timelines
Show ownership: Always use "I" instead of "we"
Focus on impact: How did your actions benefit the team/company?
Include challenges: What obstacles did you overcome?
