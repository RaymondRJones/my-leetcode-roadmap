Pro Tips for System Design Problems

  • Start with requirements - Clarify functional and non-functional requirements
  • High-level design first - Begin with major components and their interactions
  • Back-of-envelope calculations - Estimate scale, storage, and bandwidth
  • Database design - Consider data models, consistency, and partitioning
  • Scale gradually - Start simple, then discuss scaling bottlenecks
  • Consider edge cases - Discuss failure scenarios and monitoring
